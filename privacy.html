Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Avior Notes ("we," "our," or "us") is designed with a fundamental commitment to your privacy. Unlike most modern applications, we do not want your data.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers.

  • Local Storage: All notes, titles, tags, categories, and settings are stored locally in a database (SQLite) on your device.
  • Images: Images added to notes are stored in your device's local application directory. They are not uploaded to the cloud.
  • Offline Functionality: The app functions entirely offline. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

2. App Permissions

To function correctly, Avior Notes requires specific permissions on your device. We only request permissions when necessary:

  • Storage / Photos: Required to allow you to pick profile pictures and add images to your notes from your gallery.
  • Biometrics (Fingerprint/FaceID): Required to enable the "Vault" feature. Note: We do not store your biometric data. We rely on the Android/iOS system to verify your identity and return a "Success" or "Fail" result to the app.

3. Backups

  • User-Controlled: You can export your data as an .aviorbackup file.
  • Responsibility: You are solely responsible for the storage and security of your backup files (e.g., saving them to Google Drive, iCloud, or local storage). Since we do not have servers, we cannot access or recover lost backup files for you.

4. Third-Party Services

We use open-source libraries to build this app (e.g., flutter, sqflite, local_auth). These libraries run locally on your device and do not collect usage data for us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Since the app is offline, significant policy changes will likely occur only with major app updates.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via GitHub.
